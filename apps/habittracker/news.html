News & Updates

Updates to Features

New Feature: Notes Section

Take control of your habit journey with flexible note-taking! Jot down thoughts, track progress, and reflect on your habits effortlessly.

  • ✅ Flexible Note-Taking – Write notes for specific habits or general reflections under "My Day."
  • ✅ Organized Filters – View notes by All Notes, My Day, or Specific Habits.
  • ✅ Powerful Search & Date Filtering – Find past notes instantly using keywords & date ranges.
  • ✅ Edit & Delete Notes – Keep your reflections up-to-date with easy modifications.
  • ✅ Daily Note Limit for Free Users – 3 notes per day (Upgrade to Premium for unlimited access).

New Feature: Shareable Habit Streaks & Milestone Achievements

Your streaks & milestones are now worth celebrating! 🎉

  • ✅ Milestone Tracking – Celebrate your consistency with predefined streak milestones based on habit frequency.
  • ✅ Premium: Custom Milestones – Set personalized goals that matter to you! (e.g., 21 or 66 days).
  • ✅ Shareable Habit Streaks – Generate visually appealing images to share your achievements on social media.
  • ✅ One-Tap Sharing – Easily post to Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, WhatsApp, and more!
  • ✅ Encourage Habit Consistency – Milestone alerts help you stay motivated & on track.
