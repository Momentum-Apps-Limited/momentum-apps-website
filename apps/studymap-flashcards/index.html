StudyMap - Flashcards

This flashcard app is designed to help you learn vocabulary effortlessly, perfect for students, language learners, and anyone looking to boost their memory.

Why StudyMap?

100% Offline

Your data stays on your device. No internet required. Study anywhere, from the subway to the library.

Intelligent Progress Tracking

Stay on top of your goals with visual progress bars and difficulty tagging. Filter your sessions by "Learning," "Familiar," or "Confident" to ensure you are focusing your energy where it’s needed most

Simple, Clean, Modern Interface

With everything you need and nothing that you don’t. StudyMap-Flashcards provides an environment to study without distractions.
